Maha Shivratri Puja Vidhi

There is an unknown and mysterious energy that is driving all of us. Scientists have not been able
to give it a name, yet. However, saints of yore have called this unknown energy Shiva.
Shiva is the energy believed to make every living being alive. We are able to breathe,
eat, walk and carry out our day-to-day activities because of Shiva. Not only does this
energy drive living beings, but it also resides in non-living things - as their energy. Shiva, thus, drives existence.
